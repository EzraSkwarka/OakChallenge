Professor Oak's Challenge

Catch every obtainable Pokémon before earning each badge — including gift, trades, evolutions, and special encounters.

What is the challenge?
  • Before taking each Gym, obtain all Pokémon currently obtainable with legal in-game methods.
  • Evolutions that require items are required if the items are available at that point.
  • Optional: No glitches/ACE; follow your preferred community rules for dupes, gift Pokémon, and tradebacks.

This site helps track progress per game, grouped by badge and persists your progress locally in your browser.
