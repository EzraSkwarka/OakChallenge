Game Selection

Catch every obtainable Pokémon before earning each badge — including gift, trades, evolutions, and special encounters.

What is a Professor Oak Challenge?

Professor Oak has always had a dream to fill the Pokédex, but he’s too old to do it! It’s up to you as a new trainer to complete that Pokédex as much as you can independently by catching, evolving and breeding as many Pokémon as you possibly can. Professor Oak also isn’t getting any younger, so you need to complete that dex as a priority! Maybe put your gym challenge on hold for a while…

In 2018, a Reddit user named Chamale posted updates of a unique challenge on Pokémon Crystal which at the time didn’t have a name but was referred to as ‘Oak Mode’ or a ‘Professor Oak Run’. Inspired by that style of gameplay, a guide was written for FireRed while exploring how far you could “Catch ’Em All” solo or between versions of each generation without older‑gen transfers.

Core rules:

  • Catch and evolve (or breed) as many Pokémon as possible until a gym badge is required to proceed, or a required HM needs that badge. For example, fully evolve the starter and others before defeating Brock. If gyms can be taken in any order, prioritize the badge that unlocks the most Pokémon.
  • No trading with other versions. Trade evolutions are off the table, no version exclusives from the opposite version, and choices like starter or fossil are locked. In‑game NPC trades are allowed.
  • (Optional) Name the player “Oak” or “Prof. Oak”.
